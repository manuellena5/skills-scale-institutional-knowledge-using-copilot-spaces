# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA / Test Engineers

### Role Summary
QA / Test Engineers ensure quality through testing strategies, test automation, and validation of acceptance criteria. They collaborate with developers to identify issues early and maintain confidence in releases.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans and test cases
- Automate regression and integration tests
- Validate features against acceptance criteria
- Report and track defects through resolution
- Participate in release readiness reviews

### Goals
- Maximize test coverage and defect detection
- Enable fast, confident releases
- Reduce production incidents through quality gates

### Typical Communication
- Test results and coverage reports
- Defect triage in daily standups
- Release readiness sign-off

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure product usability and advocate for user needs throughout the product lifecycle. They collaborate on design specifications, user flows, and validate solutions through research and testing.

### Responsibilities
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Collaborate on user flows and interaction patterns
- Advocate for user needs during planning and review
- Validate solutions through user feedback and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize product usability and user satisfaction
- Ensure design consistency and accessibility
- Reduce friction in user journeys

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critiques
- User research findings and recommendations
- Design specs and prototypes in planning sessions

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts provide actionable insights through data collection, analysis, and visualization. They support hypothesis testing, performance reviews, and inform product decisions with evidence-based recommendations.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key metrics and KPIs
- Build dashboards and reporting tools
- Analyze user behavior and product performance
- Support A/B testing and experimentation
- Provide data-driven recommendations for product improvements

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making
- Identify opportunities and risks through data patterns
- Measure and communicate product impact

### Typical Communication
- Metric reviews and performance reports
- Data insights during planning and retrospectives
- Dashboard updates and analysis summaries

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove execution blockers, and coach teams for continuous improvement. They ensure adherence to agile practices and foster a collaborative team environment.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove impediments and blockers
- Coach team on agile principles and practices
- Shield team from disruptions and scope creep
- Track and communicate sprint progress and velocity

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and collaboration
- Ensure consistent agile practices
- Foster continuous improvement culture

### Typical Communication
- Facilitation of agile ceremonies
- Blocker escalation and resolution tracking
- Team health and velocity metrics

---

## Sponsor

### Role Summary
Sponsors provide high-level direction, ensure resource allocation, and champion the project's business value. They make strategic decisions and approve key milestones.

### Responsibilities
- Provide strategic direction and business context
- Approve budgets, resources, and major scope changes
- Champion the project with executives and stakeholders
- Review progress at key milestones
- Make go/no-go decisions on releases or pivots

### Goals
- Ensure alignment with business strategy
- Maximize return on investment
- Enable project success through support and resources

### Typical Communication
- Executive briefings and milestone reviews
- Strategic decisions and approvals
- Escalation point for critical issues

---

## System Administrator

### Role Summary
System Administrators oversee infrastructure reliability, security, and compliance. They support deployment processes, incident response, and ensure systems meet operational standards.

### Responsibilities
- Manage infrastructure, servers, and cloud resources
- Ensure security, compliance, and access controls
- Support deployment and release processes
- Respond to incidents and maintain uptime
- Monitor system health and performance

### Goals
- Maximize system reliability and uptime
- Ensure security and compliance
- Enable smooth deployments and operations

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure status and incident reports
- Deployment coordination and runbooks
- Security advisories and compliance updates
